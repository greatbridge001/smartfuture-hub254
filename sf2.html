Our Services

We offer step-by-step help for education & government processes. Each service below animates into view.

HELB / HEF Applications

Full HELB application support — first-time and repeat applicants. We compile documents and submit for you.

Requirements
  • Personal details, place of birth, resident details
  • Education background, university details
  • Parents' details, 2 guarantors, passport photo, scanned ID
  • Payment mode: Bank account or M-Pesa

KUCCPS Placement

Assist with KUCCPS course choices and revisions; guidance on selecting the best course for your career path.

Requirements
  • KCSE index number, year & grade
  • Full names (as per certificate), contact phone & email
  • Preferred courses/institutions

KMTC Placement

Support applying to KMTC programmes and required documentation for placement.

Requirements
  • KCSE index, certificate copy, National ID or Waiting Card
  • Birth certificate, valid email & phone

TSC Registration

Help teachers register with TSC, check PIN status, and submit necessary documents.

Requirements
  • KCSE/KCPE certificates, National ID
  • Passport photo, TSC form completion

KRA PIN / Registration

Assist with obtaining or updating your KRA PIN for tax purposes quickly and securely.

Requirements
  • National ID / Passport copy
  • Full legal names, contact info
  • Business or employment details if applicable

Passport Application / Renewal

Guidance for first-time passport application or renewal, with photo and document checks.

Requirements
  • Birth certificate, National ID
  • Passport photo, guardian info (if minor)
  • Fees payable via bank or M-Pesa
